Experiment 2 - Living Impressions

Concept

Description

For this experiement, I am trying to replicate a flower field, specifically a sunflower field. Someone about a sunflower field just looks so nice and calming to me and I wanted to try and replicate that. Though the initial idea was to create a lively field with moving part, in the end I was only able to make the cloud look more dynamic.

Technical

I used a function to draw a sunflower from a point to the bottom of the screen and then generate leaves, petals, and such based off of that. Then for the clouds, I created a class that would generate a cloud out of circles and have it slowly drift across the screen.

Reflection

I feel that given more time I could defnitely make this look a lot nicer and prettier. On top of that if I had the time to do more research I might be able to find a way to make it run smoother.
